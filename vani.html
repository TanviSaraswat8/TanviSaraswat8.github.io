01 · About

What is VANI?

VANI is a humanoid robot head capable of real-time voice interaction and expressive visual response. It was built entirely as a personal project — no team, no formal training, no prior hardware experience.

The project started as a curiosity: could I build something that feels alive? Something that listens, responds, and expresses? The answer turned out to be yes — and the process of figuring that out taught me more than any classroom could.

02 · Impact

What it can do.

VANI demonstrates what's possible when robotics meets human-centred design. The potential applications span across multiple sectors.

🏥
Healthcare Assistance
Companion robot for patients in hospitals or care homes — providing voice interaction where human presence is limited.
📚
Education
Interactive teaching assistant for classrooms — engaging students through expressive, voice-driven interaction.
🤝
Social Robotics
Human-robot interaction research platform — exploring how robots can communicate more naturally with people.
03 · Future

Where this is going.

VANI is a foundation. The next phase involves integrating more intelligent systems to make it truly autonomous and contextually aware.

— AI language model integration — enabling natural, multi-turn conversations beyond simple command-response patterns
— Emotion recognition — reading facial expressions to adapt responses in real time
— Clinical deployment — adapting VANI for environments where human presence is limited or high-risk
— Miniaturisation & portability — making the system lighter, wireless, and deployable in the field
